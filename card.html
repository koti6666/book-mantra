Kajal A Kitchlu (née Aggarwal;[1] born 19 June 1985[2]) is an Indian actress who works predominantly in Telugu and Tamil language films alongside Hindi language films.[3][4][5] She has worked in more than 50 films and has received two South Indian International Movie Awards.
